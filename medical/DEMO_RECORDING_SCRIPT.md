## Recording Tips

### Technical Setup:
- Screen resolution: 1920x1080 minimum
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Audio: Clear microphone, minimize background noise
- Lighting: Good illumination for mobile recording

### Presentation Style:
- Speak clearly and confidently
- Point to specific elements on screen
- Maintain steady camera/mobile position
- Keep within time limits exactly

### Post-Processing:
- Trim to exact time limits
- Add brief intro/outro if desired
- Ensure audio levels are consistent
- Export in MP4 format
