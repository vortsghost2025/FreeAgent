## Contact

## Maintainer Responsibilities

- Ensure changes comply with project constraints (no unauthorized Phase 2, no runtime enforcement without validation, no authority transfer).

- Coordinate with the Deliberate‑AI‑Ensemble public archive maintainers where relevant.
