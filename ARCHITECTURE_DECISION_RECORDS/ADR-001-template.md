# Architecture Decision Record (ADR) — Template

**Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Superseded | Deprecated  
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD  
**Author(s):**  

## Context

Describe the forces or context that motivate this decision. Include relevant technical, organizational, or external factors.

## Decision

State the chosen approach concisely. Explain what will be done and any key design details.

## Rationale

- Why this option was selected over alternatives.
- Key trade‑offs and mitigations.
- Alignment with project goals and constraints (e.g., FreeAgent is not a lane; lattice authority is supreme).

## Consequences

- **Positive**: expected benefits.
- **Negative**: known drawbacks or risks.
- **Downstream impacts**: effects on other subsystems, teams, or external dependencies.

## Implementation Notes (optional)

- Steps to implement the decision.
- Testing or validation needed.
- Rollback or migration considerations.

## Links & References

- Related issues, documents, or external resources.
